# Visa and Immigration (Guide excerpt)

## D-2 Student Visa

International degree students typically need a D-2 visa before entering Korea. Apply at the Korean embassy or consulate in your home country with the admission letter and financial documents.

## Alien Registration Card (ARC)

Within 90 days after arrival, register at the immigration office and obtain an ARC. Bring passport, photo, housing contract, and university certificate.

## Visa Extension

Before your stay period expires, apply for an extension with the International Office guidance. Late extension may result in fines.
